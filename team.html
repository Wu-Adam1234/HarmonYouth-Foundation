Meet the team

Right now, it's just me. We're growing.

HarmonYouth Foundation is started and run by students. Here's the founder, and who we're hoping joins next.

Founder

From the person who started it

"I'd been going to play piano at a senior home on my own for a while before it really hit me: the residents were never the bottleneck. I was."

I'm Adam. I study piano through the RCM program on top of regular school in Calgary. HarmonYouth started because I wanted those visits to actually happen on a schedule, with more people showing up than just me.

It's not supposed to be a one-time recital. The idea is a date people can plan around, and a place for other student musicians to play for someone besides an exam board.

If you play an instrument and want in, or you work at a home and want to set something up, send me a message.

AdamFounder, HarmonYouth Foundation
