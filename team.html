Founder

From the person who started it

I'm Adam. I started piano when I was young, and this year I finished Level 10 through the RCM program, on top of regular school here in Calgary. At some point I started caring more about playing for people than playing for a grade.

The first time I went to a senior home, it wasn't part of anything official, I just asked if I could play for an hour. People who hadn't said much the whole visit were humming along by the end. That stuck with me more than any exam result ever has.

I started HarmonYouth because I didn't want that to be something I did once and forgot about. I wanted it to be an actual schedule people could count on, and I wanted other musicians to get to feel what that afternoon felt like too.

AdamFounder, HarmonYouth Foundation
Co-Founder

Co-founded with someone who plays just as seriously

I'm Hanry. I'm RCM Level 10 on piano, and I've played clarinet for five years.

I co-founded HarmonYouth because I wanted our shows to actually run on a set schedule, with more than just two of us showing up each time.

It's not supposed to be random pop-up performances. The idea is fixed dates we commit to, so we're not just showing up unannounced one day.

If you have any questions, reach out to us.

HanryCo-Founder, HarmonYouth Foundation
