What we do

A standing performance schedule, not a one-time event.

HarmonYouth Foundation coordinates student musicians to perform at senior care homes on a recurring basis. Each show runs 30 to 45 minutes and features a rotating group of 5 to 10 performers.

The goal is consistency. Residents come to expect the next show, and student musicians get a real audience instead of a one-off recital slot.
