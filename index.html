HarmonYouth Foundation, Calgary

Senior homes don't get enough live music. We're trying to change that.

Every other week, a group of student musicians from around Calgary shows up at a senior home and plays. Piano, strings, voice, whatever people bring.

What we do

Most homes only see entertainment once in a while. We wanted to make it a regular thing.

HarmonYouth Foundation puts together student musicians who perform at senior homes on a regular schedule, not as a one-off concert. Each show runs about 30 to 45 minutes, with anywhere from 5 to 10 performers depending on who's free that week.

The point is that residents start to expect it. It becomes part of their week instead of something they forget happened by the next month. And the musicians get to play for people who actually want to listen, not a panel of judges.
